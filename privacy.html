Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This is a plain-language policy for the FORGEKIT Studio informational website. It is a general template, not legal advice. If you later add accounts, analytics, a mailing list, or on-site payments, this policy should be updated to match.

This Privacy Policy explains how FORGEKIT Studio ("FORGEKIT", "we", "us") handles information in connection with our website (the "Site"). We designed the Site to collect as little personal data as possible.

1. Information we collect

The Site is a static storefront with no user accounts, no login, and no on-site checkout. We do not collect names, addresses, or payment details on the Site. The only personal information we receive is what you choose to send us directly — for example, if you email us for support, we receive your email address and message.

2. Purchases happen off-site

When you click a "ClearlyDev" or "Roblox" button, you are taken to that marketplace to complete your purchase. Any information you provide there (including payment details) is collected and handled by that marketplace under its own privacy policy, not ours. Please review the ClearlyDev and Roblox privacy policies.

3. Hosting, fonts, and technical data

The Site is hosted on GitHub Pages and loads fonts from Google Fonts. As part of delivering any website, these providers may automatically process limited technical data such as your IP address and browser type to serve the page. We do not run our own analytics or advertising trackers on the Site.

4. Cookies

The Site itself does not set tracking or advertising cookies. Third-party services you interact with (such as the marketplaces you visit to purchase) may set their own cookies under their policies.

5. How we use information

We use any information you email us solely to respond to your request, provide support, and improve our Products. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data retention

We keep support correspondence only as long as needed to help you and to keep reasonable records, after which it may be deleted.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Children

The Site and Products are intended for developers building on Roblox and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Site.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email mmholdings7@gmail.com or reach us through our ClearlyDev store.
