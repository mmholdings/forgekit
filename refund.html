Refund Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This is a plain-language policy template for digital goods, not legal advice. Because purchases are completed on ClearlyDev and the Roblox Creator Store, those marketplaces' refund rules apply to your order and take precedence over this summary.

FORGEKIT Studio Products are digital goods that are delivered instantly and can be downloaded and copied immediately after purchase. This policy explains how refunds work.

1. Where you bought matters

All sales are processed by the marketplace you purchase from, so refunds are handled under that marketplace's policy:

  • ClearlyDev — purchases are covered by ClearlyDev's Refund Protection Guarantee. Disputes must generally be filed within 30 days of purchase. See ClearlyDev's refund terms on the product page and in your order.
  • Roblox Creator Store — purchases are subject to Roblox's applicable purchase and refund policies.

2. Because these are digital goods

Since Products are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned" once downloaded, all sales are generally considered final. We think that's fair only when the Product works as described — see the next section.

3. When we'll make it right

We want you to get what you paid for. We will help arrange a fix, replacement, or refund (subject to the marketplace's process) if:

  • the files are missing, corrupted, or won't import;
  • the Product materially does not match its description; or
  • you were charged more than once for the same order.

Please reach out within 30 days of purchase so we can investigate and, where appropriate, help you obtain a refund through the marketplace.

4. What isn't covered

Refunds are generally not available for: change of mind after downloading; lack of the skills or tools needed to use a developer asset; incompatibility caused by heavy modification of the Product; or requests made outside the marketplace's dispute window.

5. How to request help

Contact us with your order details (marketplace, product name, and order/receipt reference) and a short description of the issue. Email mmholdings7@gmail.com or message us through our ClearlyDev store. For ClearlyDev orders, you can also open a dispute directly on your order within the refund window.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
